The Invitation

A New Orbit Begins

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Tulsiramji Gaikwad-Patil College of Engineering & Technology, cordially invites you to grace the Re-Installation Ceremony of the EMULOUS Forum, marking the formal beginning of a new student body dedicated to learning, collaboration, and innovation.

We would be honoured by your gracious presence as Mrs. Vidya Raut, Lead Salesforce Developer, Persistent, Nagpur, graces the occasion as Guest of Honor and inaugurates the newly reinstalled office bearers of the forum.

Your presence would inspire our students and add distinction to this occasion.

Prof. Anita Yadav
Head of Department (CSE)
Prof. Ashwini Mahajan
Forum Coordinator (CSE)
Department of Computer Science & Engineering, TGPCET
